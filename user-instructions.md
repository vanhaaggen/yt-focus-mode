# Focus Mode - User Instructions

## Title
Focus Mode

## Summary (132 characters max)
Hide distracting elements on any website with a simple point-and-click interface. Customize your browsing experience on any site.

## Description

Focus Mode helps you create a distraction-free browsing experience by allowing you to hide unwanted elements on any website. Simply click on elements you want to remove, and the extension remembers your preferences for each site you visit.

Whether you're trying to block annoying sidebars, intrusive ads, recommended content sections, or any other distracting elements, Focus Mode gives you complete control over what you see on every website.

**Key Features:**

- **Point-and-Click Hiding**: Enter selection mode and click on any element to hide it instantly
- **Site-Specific Memory**: Your hidden elements are saved per website and persist across visits
- **Real-Time Tracking**: See how many elements are hidden on the current site
- **Undo Functionality**: Quickly restore the last hidden element if you make a mistake
- **Manage Hidden Elements**: View and individually remove items from your hidden elements list
- **Export & Import**: Save your configuration and transfer it between browsers or devices
- **Reset Options**: Clear settings for a single site or reset all sites at once

Focus Mode works on any website, giving you the power to customize your browsing experience without needing technical knowledge or CSS selectors. Just point, click, and hide.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to any website where you want to hide distracting elements
2. Click the Focus Mode extension icon in your browser toolbar
3. Click "Start Selection Mode" to begin selecting elements
4. Hover over and click any element on the page you want to hide
5. The element will be hidden immediately and saved for future visits
6. Exit selection mode by clicking the extension icon again or pressing Escape

Your preferences are automatically saved and will apply every time you visit the same website.

## Privacy

Focus Mode stores all your settings locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers. The extension only requires permissions to modify web pages and store your preferences locally.
